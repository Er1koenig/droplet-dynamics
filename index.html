Project Overview

This project augments an in‑house Lattice Boltzmann solver with an Euler–Lagrange module that tracks deformable, sub‑Kolmogorov droplets and bubbles. The model conserves volume, resolves shape evolution via the Maffettone–Minale formulation, and supports two‑way momentum coupling.

  • Novel correction term eliminates rotation‑induced geometry error in pure vortex flow.
  • Validation in quiescent, shear and vortex tests; benchmarked an oblate rising bubble.
  • Direct numerical simulation of droplet‑laden isotropic turbulence.
